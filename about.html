About World Scholarship Finder

World Scholarship Finder is a free resource dedicated to helping students, researchers, and professionals find scholarships for international study.

Our mission is to make global education opportunities accessible to everyone by listing trusted scholarships from around the world and providing helpful tips for applying.

Who We Are

This project is maintained by dagimmanyazewal38-stack and the open-source community. We believe that everyone deserves a chance at global education and career development.

How It Works

  • Search for scholarships by keyword, country, level, or field.
  • Browse tips and resources for successful applications.
  • Suggest scholarships or opportunities to help others.

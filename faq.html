Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I find scholarships for my country or field?

Use the filters on the homepage to search by country, level (Undergraduate, Graduate, PhD), and field of study. You can also use keywords in the search bar.

Is World Scholarship Finder free to use?

Yes! Our resource is completely free for students, researchers, and anyone seeking scholarship opportunities.

How often is the scholarship list updated?

We strive to keep the list up-to-date. New scholarships are added regularly, and expired ones are removed as soon as possible. Users can suggest scholarships using the Suggest page.

Do you offer application support?

We provide tips and guides on the Tips page, but we do not process applications or offer one-on-one support.

Can I suggest a scholarship?

Yes! Use the Suggest page to submit new opportunities for review.

How can I contact the team?

Visit the Contact page to send us a message or feedback.
